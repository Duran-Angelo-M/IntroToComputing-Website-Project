The name Mlang
is a legend

Mlang is from the root word Tamlang which means “full of Bamboos” and it is originated from the Maguindanaoan language. The bario of Mlang was considered as part of the Buluan before. Maguindanaoan tribe was the first inhabitants in the bario of Tamlang/Mlang lead by Datu Inong.
